What is it about?

Other Side is an animated adult series that follows the misadventures of Rey (A Red Fox who is very shy, innocent and introverted) and her roommate Chloe (An Arctic Fox who is carefree, outgoing and overall a bad influence) who live in the city of Brooklyn, New York, in which they face problems of such a big city, as well as dealing with other issues such as annoying people, work, social, emotional and adult life problems.
