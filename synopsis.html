Otherside is an animated web series that follows the misadventures of Rey and Chloe, two roomates that share an apartment in the city of New York, USA.

Rey is a polite, shy, introverted girl which works at the local Coffe Shop.

On the other hand, Chloe is a carefree, social and all around troublemaker.
