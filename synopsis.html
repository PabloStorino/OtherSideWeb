What is it about?

OtherSide is an animated adult series about the misadventures of Rey, a very shy and naive Red Fox that works at a Coffe Shop (voiced by Valeria Cuéllar) and her rommate Chloe, a carefree outgoing troublemaker (voiced by Carmen Mazariegos).

Both of them reside in the city of Brooklyn, New York. Dealing with daily life adult problems such as annoying people, work stress, social anxiety and emotional problems.
